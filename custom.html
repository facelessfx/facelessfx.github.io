I take on custom pedal design and build commissions. Whether you're looking for a different take on an existing design or have an idea for something totally out of the ordinary, I'm ready to work with you on a unique project.

While most of my work has been with vintage style fuzz pedals, I am also experienced with the design of other analog musical effects such as frequency boosters, distortion, filters, delay and modulation.

  • Services
  • Realisation of your pedal idea
  • Circuit design from concept to schematic to build
  • Rendering of existing circuits into new layouts
  • Construction on tagboard, stripboard (vero), PCB, or point-to-point
  • Graphic design - images and text for various finishing methods
  • Etching of enclosures for your own DIY project
